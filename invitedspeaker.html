Dr. Gaurav Aggarwal

Staff Research Scientist Google Research Bengaluru, Karnataka

    A Computer Science B. Tech. from IIT Madras and PhD from University of Maryland, Gaurav has over fifteen years  of  experience  in  building computer vision and machine learning solutions both in academic and industrial   settings.   Gaurav   has   around   50   research   papers   in   international conferences  and  journals,  he  has  written as part of his research career.
