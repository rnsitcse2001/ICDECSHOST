Instructions for Paper Submission

Each paper will be blind reviewed by two prominent researchers in the relevant area. For each of the accepted papers, one of the authors need to be registered.

Instructions for Tutorial Proposals

The tutorial proposals for about 2 hours on the topics related to the conference theme with a brief abstract, intended audience and outline should be sent to the Organizing Secretary before the deadline. The research scholars interested in attending only the tutorials are to be registered.
